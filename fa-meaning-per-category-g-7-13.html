Meaning per category - G

These signs indicate the beginning or end of certain areas, roads and paths. In these areas and on these roads or paths there are sometimes additional admission requirements or other extra rules may apply.

There are a number of differences between the mandatory cycle path (sign G-11) and the non-mandatory cycle path (G-13):

  • If there is a mandatory cycle path, cyclists and snorfietsers may not use the carriageway. They have to ride on the cycle path.

  • If there is a non-mandatory cycle path, cyclists may choose between the cycle path and the carriageway.

  • Snorfietsers are only allowed to use a non-mandatory cycle path if they have their combustion engine switched off. An electric motor may be switched on.

A number of signs in this category can also be used as zone signs. These signs remain valid until the end zone sign.
