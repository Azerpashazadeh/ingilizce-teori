Supplementary plates

By use of supplementary plates, the signs above those plates can be clarified, limited or extended.

All these plates can also be used with the word ‘uitgezonderd’ (excepted). In that case, the sign above does not apply to the displayed category. Instead, it applies to all other categories.

These supplementary plates will be used with priority signs. The thick line indicates the divergent priority road. The thin lines indicate the side roads. Drivers on the side roads must give right of way to drivers on the divergent priority road.
