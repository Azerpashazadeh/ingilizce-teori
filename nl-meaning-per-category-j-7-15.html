Meaning per category - J

This category consists entirely of warning signs. These signs give you the opportunity to anticipate a possible danger in time. Sometimes they are combined with category A signs, this can be with a speed limit or with a recommended speed.

St. Andrew’s crosses are always displayed at level crossings (indicated by signs J-10 and J-11). These indicate how many tracks cross the road. With a single St. Andrew’s cross (J-12) this is only one, with a double St. Andrew’s cross this can be two, but also more. In that case, after a train has passed, a second train can also come from the opposite direction.
