Meaning per category - E

This category is all about parking and stopping vehicles and placing bicycles, mopeds, snorfietsen and speed pedelecs.

This includes the following signs, which indicate that parking or waiting is prohibited on that side of the road , except in the designated areas:

The E-1 sign only applies to drivers of motor vehicles and brommobielen.
The E-3 sign only applies to riders of bicycles, snorfietsen, mopeds and speed pedelecs.

The following signs indicate that parking is allowed. These signs often state to whom the sign applies. Some signs also indicate how you can park, or whether you have to pay for parking. If you do not meet these requirements, you may not park there.

Signs in this category can also be designed as zone signs. These signs remain valid until the sign end zone. They are valid throughout the zone on both sides of the road.
